## Interaction Guide

### How to Navigate the Dashboard

1. **Login** - Enter your credentials to access the dashboard
2. **Home Page** - View quick access shortcuts and account status
3. **Dashboard** - Access detailed analytics and business metrics
4. **Saldo** - Manage your account balance and payments
5. **Other Features** - Use sidebar menu to access additional features

### Common Tasks

#### Verifying Your Account
- Click "Verifikasi Akun" button
- Accept terms and conditions
- Provide required information

#### Managing Balance
- Go to Saldo menu
- View current balance
- Add bank account information
- Withdraw funds when needed

#### Creating Orders
- Click "Order form" in quick access
- Fill in order details
- Submit form
- Track order status in dashboard

#### Buying Credits
- Click "Beli Kredit" button
- Select credit amount (100K, 200K, 500K, 1M)
- Confirm purchase
- Credits will be added to account

#### Creating Promotions
- Click "Tambah promo" in quick access
- Enter promotion details
- Set discount/offer
- Launch promotion
