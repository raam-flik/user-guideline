## TROUBLESHOOTING & FAQ

### Q: Can't see my balance?
A: Make sure you're on the Saldo page. Your balance may be loading. Refresh the page if needed.

### Q: How do I withdraw money?
A: Go to the Saldo page and use the Pencairan (Withdrawal) form. Enter amount and submit.

### Q: What does GMV mean?
A: GMV = Gross Merchandise Value. It's your total transaction volume on the Dashboard page.

### Q: How do I buy credits?
A: Click the "Beli Kredit" button and select your desired amount (100K, 200K, 500K, or 1M).

### Q: How to verify my account?
A: Click "Verifikasi Akun" button on the Home page, accept terms, and submit.

### Q: Can I create multiple promotions?
A: Yes, click "Tambah promo" each time you want to create a new promotion.

### Q: Where are my transactions?
A: View transactions in the Saldo page under the transaction history table.

### Q: What fees apply?
A: See the breakdown in Saldo page: PG fee, Recollection fee, Commission, etc.
